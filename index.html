Welcome to Game Design

“Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn.”

Benjamin Franklin

During the two semesters of this course, we will discuss the game design process, the history of video games, explore the many exciting job opprotunities in this field, and begin building our own games. We will learn what makes a great game, what elements go into a great game, and the nitty gritty of creating one yourself. We will learn about concepts in programming, and some aspects of C# which also apply to many other computer programming languages. We get to learn about art and how art is important to game design. How are games marketed, and why must gameplay be balanced? We will utilize the Unity Game Engine to power our platformer and 3D games.

On this website, you can find information about the course, unit resources, student examples, LiveLesson recordings, and more.
